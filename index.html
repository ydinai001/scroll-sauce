Frequently Asked

What footage do I need to provide?
Send us your raw clips in any format (MP4, MOV, etc.). The more footage options you give us, the better the final edit. Include any specific shots you definitely want included and let us know your vision.
How does the revision process work?
You get one round of revisions included with each reel. After we deliver your first cut, you can request changes to timing, music, captions, or any other elements. We'll make the tweaks within 24 hours.
What about music licensing?
We use trending sounds from Instagram and TikTok's music libraries, so you're always covered for in-platform posting. If you need royalty-free music for other uses, just let us know.
Can I get bulk pricing?
Yes! We offer packages for creators who need regular content. Order 5+ reels at once and get 15% off. Monthly retainer plans are also available - just ask us about custom pricing.
What's included in the 3-day turnaround?
3 business days from when you submit your footage and we confirm receipt. Rush delivery (24-48 hours) is available for +$20 if you need it faster.
