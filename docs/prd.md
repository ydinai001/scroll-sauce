# Scroll Sauce - Product Requirements Document

**Status**: Draft
**Last Updated**: December 2024

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Scroll Sauce** is a short-form video editing service for content creators who want professional, scroll-stopping reels without the editing hassle.

### Value Proposition
Transform raw footage into viral-worthy content with professional editing, transitions, captions, and music.

---

## 2. Service Offering

### Package: Single Reel Edit

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Price** | $40 per reel |
| **Turnaround** | 3 business days |
| **Max Length** | Up to 60 seconds |

### Included Features
- Professional editing & cuts
- Smooth transitions
- Captions/subtitles
- Licensed music selection
- 1 revision round

### Target Platforms
- Instagram Reels
- TikTok

---

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Persona: Emerging Content Creator
- Posts 2-5x per week
- Growing audience (1K-50K followers)
- Time-constrained
- Values quality but can't afford full-time editor
- Platforms: Instagram, TikTok

### Pain Points
- Editing is time-consuming
- Inconsistent quality affects growth
- Don't know trending edit styles
- Music licensing confusion

---

## 4. Business Platform

### Service Delivery: HoneyBook
- Intake form for project details
- Contract management
- Invoicing & payments
- Client communication
- File delivery

### Workflow
1. Client submits inquiry via landing page
2. Intake questionnaire collects project details
3. Contract + invoice sent automatically
4. Client uploads raw footage
5. Editor delivers within 3 days
6. Client reviews, requests revision if needed
7. Final delivery
